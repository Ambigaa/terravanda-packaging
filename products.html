Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our products

What's the minimum order quantity?

MOQs vary by product: 100 units for boxes, 500 for mailers, 250 for bags. Custom projects start at 500 units. We offer sample packs for testing.

How long does shipping take?

Standard production takes 7-10 business days, plus shipping. Express options available. We offset carbon for all deliveries.

Can I get samples before ordering?

Yes! Request a sample kit through our contact form. Sample packs include 3-5 product samples for $25 (refundable with first order).

Are your products food-safe?

Most of our products are food-safe and FDA-compliant. Check individual product specs or ask our team for food-grade certifications.
