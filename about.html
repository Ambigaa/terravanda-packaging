We're on a Mission to Eliminate Single-Use Plastic

TerraVanda was founded to make sustainable packaging accessible, affordable, and beautiful for every brand.

OUR STORY

From a Garage to a Movement

It started in 2020 when our founder, Ambigaa, realized how much packaging waste her e-commerce business was generating. She searched for sustainable alternatives but found options that were either too expensive, low quality, or required massive minimum orders.

So she decided to create her own. What began as a small operation in her garage has grown into a trusted supplier for over 500 brands across India.
